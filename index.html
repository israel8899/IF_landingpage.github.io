Israel First – Our Plan to Put Citizens First

Putting Israel and its citizens first: jobs, homes, security, and sovereignty.

1. Domestic Labor & Employment

The backbone of Israel is its people. Big corporations and bought politicians flood our country with foreign labor, driving down wages and taking jobs from Israelis. Enough.

  • Phase out foreign labor in sectors where Israelis are available within five years.
  • Create 200,000 new domestic jobs through vocational training, apprenticeships, and industrial development.
  • Launch a Young Immigrant Workforce Initiative: attract 50,000 young immigrants to join the labor force in the first three years.
  • Provide tax incentives for businesses that maintain 80%+ Israeli staff; penalize wage suppression through foreign labor.

2. Housing & Generational Wealth

Young Israelis work hard, pay rent, and still cannot afford to own a home. Meanwhile, the powerful hoard land and build overpriced apartments.

  • Build 20,000 single-family homes in sprawling new communities across Israel & Judea & Samaria.
  • Grant long-term land rights for citizens in new development zones.
  • Launch a National Housing Fund for first-time buyers with subsidized mortgage rates.
  • Ensure transparent tenders and anti-speculation policies to prevent price inflation.
