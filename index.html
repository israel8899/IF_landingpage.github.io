Israel First – Our Plan to Put Citizens First

Putting Israel and its citizens first: jobs, homes, security, and sovereignty.

Jobs for Israelis

  • Phase out foreign labor in key sectors
  • Create 200,000 new domestic jobs
  • Attract young immigrants to strengthen the workforce
  • Provide incentives for businesses hiring Israelis first

Homes for Every Family

  • Build 20,000 single-family homes in new communities
  • Grant long-term land ownership for citizens
  • Launch a National Housing Fund for first-time buyers
  • Prevent speculation and monopolies

Economic Independence

  • Invest in hi-tech, manufacturing, and defense industries
  • Revive the Israeli jet fighter program
  • Support domestic startups creating local jobs
  • Expand domestic agriculture for self-reliance

Sovereignty & Integration

  • Dismantle the Palestinian Authority; unified control from Jordan River to Mediterranean
  • Prioritize strategic land development for security and population integration
  • Build railways and highways connecting all regions
  • Evaluate Ben Gurion Canal for trade and security

National Security & Defense

  • Build an advanced Israeli jet fighter
  • Expand defense tech R&D: cyber, unmanned, missile defense
  • Strengthen domestic military manufacturing
  • Coordinate national defense for critical infrastructure

Geopolitical Independence

  • Diversify diplomatic and trade relations
  • Lead a Democratic Alliance of Nations
  • Exclude authoritarian regimes from influence
  • Build partnerships with emerging powers

Anti-Corruption & Transparency

  • Independent auditing of ministries and institutions
  • Public disclosure of foreign donations and contracts
  • Whistleblower protections for corruption reporting

National Unity

  • Bridge secular, religious, urban, and rural communities
  • Implement youth and veteran programs linking service to employment
  • Foster mutual respect and shared responsibility

Infrastructure & Connectivity

  • Develop railways and highways connecting all regions
  • Expand water, energy, and communications infrastructure
  • Create domestic jobs through strategic infrastructure projects
