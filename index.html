Territorial Sovereignty & Strategic Depth
Israel First supports full legal and administrative integration of all territories under Israeli control. This integration will provide strategic depth, coordinated infrastructure planning, and expanded opportunities for housing and economic development under unified governance.
Housing & Home Ownership
Home ownership is a cornerstone of family stability and long-term wealth. The party will accelerate land release, streamline planning, and fast-track construction programs prioritized for Israeli citizens and families.
Labor, Wages & Domestic Employment
We will roll out a multi-year plan to replace foreign labor with Israeli workers, increase wages in affected sectors, and deploy nationwide vocational training to ensure economic mobility.
Immigration & Integration
Focused immigration of younger skilled migrants will be encouraged, with integration services that place new arrivals into work and training quickly to support national labor needs.
Defense Technology & Sovereign Industry
The state will invest in advanced defense systems, manufacturing, and dual-use technologies to ensure economic resilience and strategic autonomy. Critical industries will be anchored under Israeli ownership and oversight.
Independent Auditing & Foreign Influence
Independent third-party audits will review governmental activity for corruption and external influence. Findings will be public, and recommendations implemented to protect Israel’s sovereignty and public trust.
